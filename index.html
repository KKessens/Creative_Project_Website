Story of Food

Food has always been a big part of my life. I love trying new things and I’ve never been a picky eater (except for cheese). The foods I’ve eaten often have a story behind them, especially if it’s something new. Below are foods that I thought had an interesting context, whether it’s because I was in a new place, I wanted to try something new, or some other reason.

Kansas City is known as the haunted house capital of the world. Being from there, I’ve visited them a few times. In 2018, I visited the Beast, one of the more famous houses, which had a line that stretched for almost two blocks. In the meantime, we got some fried Oreos to try. I couldn’t finish all of them, even though there were only four, because of all the salt and sugar. This is a picture of one of the leftovers.

I never cared much for my high school cafeteria's food. The portions were small with a scarce amount of flavor. However, there was one item on the menu that almost everyone agreed was the best, the Asian Bowl. It was just fried rice with orange chicken but everyone looked forward to it on Fridays. Looking back, it probably wasn’t that good, but since everyone was so hungry by lunchtime it was put on an inflated pedestal.

Every year, my family and I go to Colorado to ski. Usually we go to Keystone, but in 2020 we instead went to Breckenridge. On the trip, however, I got sick and wasn’t sure if I would be able to ski. Usually when I’m sick, I eat as much food as I can hoping that I’ll feel better the next day. I tried that here where I ordered a double bison bacon burger, the biggest thing on the menu. The waitress was shocked when I finished the whole thing. The next day I woke up and felt normal. This was in the earlier stages of the pandemic, but I don’t think I caught COVID since I remember how rich the burger was.

My mom’s from Barcelona, so we usually visit there annually to catch up with family. I’m from Kansas, which makes it hard to appreciate seafood. Whenever I go to Spain, I try to eat as much as I can. This was my first time eating a fish served like this, since I was used to eating fish that had already been cut. However, the taste was magnitudes richer than anything I’d had in Kansas and one of my favorite fishes that I’ve had.

Chicago was a city that I often passed through while visiting colleges. Everytime I did, I’d always get deep dish pizza. I have a large appetite that makes it difficult for any single meal to completely fill me up. Deep dish pizza is an exception, making it something I always get whenever I have the chance.

Earlier I mentioned how I visit Spain annually to catch up with my family. My favorite food from Spain is Paella, which makes sense given how much I love seafood. Everything in it is made from some sort of seafood, whether it's the sauce, mussels, or shrimp. I had the paella shown in this picture right next to a beach with almost my entire extended family.

I’ve been to Disney World three times, with my favorite part being Galaxy’s Edge, an entire area dedicated to Star Wars. I’ve been a fan of Star Wars for as long as I can remember and as I’ve gotten older I’ve only grown to like it more. This drink was one that I had in the Star Wars based cantina and I remember it as being one of the most unique and exciting moments of that trip.

I started fencing when I was seven and have been doing it ever since. In 2023, I went to Denver to fence in the Junior Olympics. Afterwards, I went out with my mom and my second cousins to get Sushi. I don’t get to see my second cousins very often so it was fun to go out and have some food with them.

Ask any of my friends and they’ll tell you how much I love dessert and sweets. At the top of my pyramid is this: the Cinnabon Cinnamon Swirl Cheesecake from the Cheesecake Factory. I had this after one of my brother's tennis tournaments in downtown Kansas City with my family and grandparents. I’ll never forget how this tasted. It was a blend of every flavor that I love: sweets, cinamon, cream cheese, whipped cream, and caramel. Amazing.

When it comes to my favorite restaurants in Madison, two immediately come to my mind: The Globe and Estacion Inka. Usually, I go to Inka and always get the same thing: Pollo Tropical with double chicken and cilantro rice. Earlier I mentioned how I have a huge appetite and this always leaves me feeling satisfied. The chicken is always perfectly cooked making it one of the best chickens I’ve had. I always eat here after an exam, if I’m feeling too lazy to make something, or if I just want some good food. No matter what, I always feel better after eating here.

Living in an apartment instead of a dorm meant that I needed to start making my own food. The first thing that I decided to make was spaghetti with ground beef. In this particular meal, I had some corn that I bought at the farmers market. Spaghetti is one of the more common foods that I make since it's easy to add whatever I want. However, this was the first time that I made it in my apartment so I remember this meal fondly.

My freshmen year, I was excited when I heard UW-Madison had a fencing club. I immediately joined and made some close friends. In October of 2024, we went to a tournament at Northwestern where we went out to eat in Chicago. We all did pretty well and went to Chinatown for some Dim Sum. The food was amazing and I can’t wait to go back next year.

I have the same roomate now as I did last year. We immediately got along and have been close friends since. We always talked about making steaks while in our dorm room and now that we had an apartment, we finally got around to it. We also made some fried rice to go with it. It was lots of fun and the steak turned out well.
